Sudaset

Based in the heart of the Sudanese Communities in the United States and Canada, SudaSet is a trans-continental foundation connecting the Greater Sudanese Region (Sudan, South Sudan, and neighboring countries in the Horn of Africa) with hubs of developmental, social, and economic organizations and entities, working both internationally and regionally.

Our Vision

To facilitate and accelerate the Region’s march towards socioeconomic development through supporting public, private sector, and grassroots civil organizations’ initiatives, both in the region and abroad.

Our Mission

In today’s interconnected world, SudaSet aim to embolden international cooperation and mutual understanding (both on governmental & community levels) to nurture the prosperity and well-being of the peoples of the region. We focus on strengthening effective communication, and supporting exchange of knowledge and experience, to help the region overcome both the humanitarian and developmental issues that have long impeded its progress towards sustained growth, development, and prosperity.

Our Partners

SudaSet is a partner for like-minded organizations working for the welfare of the Greater Sudanese Region and its peoples, including government agencies, NGOs and activists in social change, academia, and media. SudaSet is committed to partner with all organizations and individuals who believe in direct-impact partnerships, for the benefit of local communities and the thriving civil society in the greater Sudan area.

Our Work

Linking governmental and non-governmental institutions, public and private sector, and civil society organizations in the United States and Canada, with their counterparts in the Greater Sudanese Region.

Connecting Development Agencies, NGOs and organizations in the United States and Canada, with social and professional bodies inside the region and abroad.

Utilizing Greater Sudan’s civil society and their activism, expertise, and deep local knowledge, in guiding and supporting international projects activities in the region.

Strengthening and invigorating the regions’ communities (including social groups, professionals, and business organizations) in the United States and Canada to help maximize their contribution in the development of the Greater Sudanese Region.

Leadership, Team and Partners

SudaSet Team is a working group of professionals actively engaged in socioeconomic development the wider region and abroad. Many of our professionals in the field come from grassroots activism and hands-on project experience, with documented practical awareness on the ground, dealing with the most pressing issues affecting local communities.

Our team is committed to seek new perspectives and approaches in solving problems often thought of in traditional terms; they are always inspired and guided by local wisdom and deep understanding, rooted in the experience of the beneficiaries of community development and change.

SudaSet employs a group of experts from different nationalities who are highly skilled in providing consultancy, project design, implementation and evaluation in various fields and topics.

Services

Professional Services

Our aim is to play the role of a trusted provider of a wide range of services essential for the success of ambitious developmental and social programs and projects in the region. These may include advisory services, technical support, and professional training programs for: Governmental and non-governmental organizations and their staff; Donors and international organizations; Project Managers and Field workers in civil society organizations and all entities working extensively in the Greater Sudanese region.

SudaSet offers advisory services, technical support programs, and specialized consultations in the following areas

  • Specialized studies and field research to support intercontinental cooperation, and effective project design and management in countries of the region, to support work in fields of development, democracy, and peace.
  • Networking, developing relations, creating, and facilitating opportunities for cooperation in training, exchange of experiences and joint work.
  • Playing an integral part in making information available in the public domain, and in support of specific projects’ needs, to help develop interactive, effective platforms for trusted, accessible regional knowledge.
  • Producing specialized on-request reports on a variety of areas of common interests, especially regional development, civil society, and culture.
  • In collaboration with our in-house media and language specialist partner, South-North Media & Language Arts, SudaSet provides a team of media professional, translators and linguists who produce both original and localized media product to support effective communication.

Organizing and supporting conferences, workshops, and seminars

SudaSet provides assistance and support, through direct contracting, in designing and implementing a variety of events such as conferences, workshops, and public meetings and seminars.

Technical and General Translation Services

  • Translating project proposals, studies, research, and periodic reports
  • Translating periodicals and specialized publications
  • Translating joint conferences, workshops, and meetings
  • Translate and localize websites and social media content
